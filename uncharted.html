Uncharted 4

Uncharted 4: A Thief's End is an upcoming action-adventure third-person shooter platform video game published by Sony Computer Entertainment and developed by Naughty Dog.

It has also been announced that this will be the last entry in the Uncharted series featuring the character of Nathan Drake.
