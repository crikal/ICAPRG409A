Star Wars Battlefront

Star Wars Battlefront is an upcoming first person action video game based on the Star Wars franchise. The third major release in the Star Wars: Battlefront series.

Ea has announced that this installment won't feature space battles, multiple military classes and it will only support 32v32 instead of battlefront 2's 100 person servers.
