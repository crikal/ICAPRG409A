Tom Clancy's The Division

Tom Clancy's The Division is an upcoming open world third-person shooter role-playing video game with survival elements.

This installment will feature much like it's predecessor but it will include a longer storyline with more "interactivity".

Information

Publisher

1986- Present

Ubisoft Annecy

Other Developers: Ubisoft Massive, Ubisoft Reflections and Red Storm Entertainment

Platforms etc.

Playstation 4, Xbox One and Windows

It was announced during Ubisoft's E3 2013 press conference, together with a seven-minute gameplay demo and is set to release on March 8, 2016.
